Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Ama, a UI/UX designer based in London who loves to find oppurtunities to create valuable solutions in everyday things.

I’ve always been fascinated by the human body, how we interact with the world around us and how I can influence and make positive changes in human health. I studied BSc Biomedical Science and developed an appriciation for scientific research during my industrial placement year. I enjoy gathering data, forming, and testing ideas, evaluating outcomes and implementing- bringing a vision to life.

After spending 5+ years as a Research Scientist, I’ve decided to channel my passion for creativity, skills in visual problem solving and curious mindset into the world of UI/UX Design. My role as a Research Scientist has equipped me with an assortment of skills including, unique perspective in research processes, good communication, attention to detail and empathy.
